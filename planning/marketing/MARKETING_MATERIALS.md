## 6. Press Release

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Casebriefr Launches AI-Powered Legal Briefing Tool, Transforming How Law Students and Legal Professionals Research Cases**

*New platform generates professional case briefs from PDF court decisions in 60 seconds*

[CITY], March 11, 2026 — Casebriefr today announced the launch of its AI-powered legal briefing platform, designed to save law students, paralegals, and attorneys hours of manual research time. The tool transforms court decision PDFs into structured, professional case briefs in under a minute.

Unlike existing study aid platforms that offer pre-written briefs for select popular cases, Casebriefr can analyze any court decision — including obscure rulings, recent decisions, and state-specific cases that professors frequently assign but study guides don't cover.

"Law students spend 15-20 hours per week on case reading and briefing," said Danielle, founder of Casebriefr and a current law student. "I built Casebriefr because I was drowning in reading lists. Now I can brief an entire semester's cases in a few hours instead of a few weeks."

**How It Works:**
Users upload a PDF of any court decision to Casebriefr. The platform's AI extracts and analyzes the text, then generates a structured brief including Facts, Issues, Holding, Rule, and Reasoning — all formatted professionally and ready for class or case files.

**Key Features:**
• Upload any court decision PDF
• Receive structured brief in 60 seconds
• Download as PDF or copy text
• Batch processing for multiple cases (Pro tier)
• API access for law firms and research platforms (Pro tier)

**Pricing:**
Casebriefr operates on a freemium model. Users receive 5 free briefs per month. The Student tier ($9/month) offers unlimited briefs, while the Pro tier ($29/month) adds batch processing and API access.

**About Casebriefr:**
Casebriefr is a legal technology company founded in 2026 by a law student who experienced firsthand the time burden of manual case briefing. The company's mission is to make legal research faster and more accessible for students and professionals alike.
